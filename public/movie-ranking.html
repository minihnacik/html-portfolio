The Best Movies According to Albert

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception (2010) – Directed by Christopher Nolan

I love this movie for its complex plot that blends science fiction with psychological dilemmas. "Inception" pulls you into a world of dreams, where the line between reality and illusion is blurred, and every perception has a deeper meaning. I appreciate how it makes you think about the nature of reality.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994) – Directed by Frank Darabont

This is a classic that touches and inspires. The story of friendship in the brutal environment of a prison, hope, and the pursuit of freedom is moving and shows how even in the darkest moments, one can find meaning in life. It's a film that always leaves you with a sense of strength and survival. event.

Interstellar (2014) – Directed by Christopher Nolan

My love for space, human emotions, and sacrifice are the main reasons why this film is one of my favorites. "Interstellar" combines deep questions about humanity’s future with the personal choices of its characters. It’s also visually stunning, showcasing incredible cosmic phenomena.
